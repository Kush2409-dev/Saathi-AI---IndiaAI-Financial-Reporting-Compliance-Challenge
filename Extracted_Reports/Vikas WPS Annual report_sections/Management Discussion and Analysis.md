INDUSTRY STRUCTURE & DEVELOPMENT

The Company VIKAS WSP was established in 1988. The Company is one of the India's foremost guar gum powder (GGP) manufacturer, supplying to all sectors of the food industry with an extensive range of quality products. Besides food, the Company also offers guar gum for technical applications such as pet food, oil drilling and fracturing, textile printing, paper making, etc. Per annum production capacity of all grades guar gum powder is 58800 MT. The Company is committed to provide its customers overall services and values that is the best. It has contributed significantly to our national output, employment and exports.

COVID-19 IMPACT ON THE COMPANY

The outbreak of the COVID-19 pandemic, followed by the lockdowns in the country, has affected the business operations of the Company. The Ministry of Home Affairs vide order No. 40-3/2020 dated March 24, 2020 notified the first ever nationwide lockdown in India to restrict the spread of COVID- 19 pandemic. As a result of continuous lockdowns, the Company's operations were suspended from March 24, 2020 to April 20, 2020. On receipt of necessary approvals / permissions and after considering the Advisories & Guidelines issued by Central & State Governments, availability of labour and logistical constraints, your Company resumed partially the production activities w.e.f. April 20, 2020. The operations at the Corporate office of the Company also resumed w.e.f. April 20, 2020.
